Job Search Mobile App – UX Case Study

Designed a mobile app that enables users to easily create profiles, explore job opportunities, and apply with a smooth and guided experience.

Problem: Users experienced difficulty in completing onboarding and navigating job listings, leading to confusion and drop-offs.

Solution: Introduced a clear step-by-step onboarding flow and simplified job browsing with structured layout and intuitive navigation.

Design Approach: Focused on a guided user journey — from sign up, profile completion, and job discovery to final success feedback — using a clean and minimal interface.

Outcome: Improved user experience with smoother navigation, reduced confusion, and a faster job application process.
